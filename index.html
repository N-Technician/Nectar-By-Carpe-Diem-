About Nectar by Carpe Diem

Your Premier Rooftop Destination in Kathmandu

Welcome to Nectar by Carpe Diem, nestled in the vibrant heart of Thamel, Kathmandu. We are more than just a restaurant—we are a destination for unforgettable experiences.

Perched above the bustling streets, our rooftop venue offers breathtaking 360° valley views that transform every moment into something magical. Whether you're seeking a romantic sunset dinner, a celebration with friends, or a business gathering, Nectar is your sanctuary.
